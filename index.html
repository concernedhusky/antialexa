Northeastern's Alexa Program is a nonconsensual violation of student privacy, and must be discontinued immediately.

  • Amazon Alexas listen to you 24/7, even when you do not activate them by saying "Alexa"
  • This data is stored permanently by Amazon, and has been used in court as evidence against unwittingly surveilled defendants. Obviously, in the linked case, it's great that a guilty person was caught and jailed. But it is clearly concerning that Alexas can serve as a permanent wiretap, circumventing a warrant.
  • These Alexas are being used to help Amazon market to college students better by listening to your conversations. They are using advanced data science to manipulate you.
  • These Alexas were a wasteful misuse of your tuition dollars, as the University prioritizes serving its corporate donors over funding much-needed services such as UHCS that continually go underfunded.
